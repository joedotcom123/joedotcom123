Projects

Tambour Jewellery Box

Inspired by the tambour door mechanism, this project was made out of 3mm laser-cut MDF, fabric, and balsawood. It features a sliding tambour door that opens by pulling out one of the draws, revealing extra storage on top. The box was designed for my girlfriend to store her jewelry in, providing a unique and functional storage solution.

Magic: The Gathering Deckbox

Looking for a way to store my Magic: The Gathering decks, I pivoted towards designing a solution tailored to my needs. This project has taught me a great deal about 3D modelling and 3D printing, and is something I am currently working on.

Magic: The Gathering Storage

Making use of my experience using laser cutters, I am currently prototyping a storage solution for my Magic: The Gathering bulk. One of the main features of this project is a divider system that allows for easy organisation of cards, and is expandable to accommodate growing collections.
